The story behind the plate — About Us

Feenie's Table is a design-led brand rooted in culture, function, and story. What began with one plate — created to honour the way we actually eat — has grown into a movement. A celebration of tableware, rituals, and collaborations that centre the beauty of our traditions and the stories they hold.

We design for bold, textured meals. For hosts who lead with heart. For cultures too often left out of the design conversation. Every piece is made with intention — to be useful, to be beautiful, and to quietly reclaim space.This isn't just about what's on the table. It's about who's finally being seen at it.
